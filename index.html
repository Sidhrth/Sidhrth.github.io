Research Projects

CASEbot

Conversational AI for Self-Experimentation

Designed an LLM-powered conversational agent to guide users through designing structured, personalized, and safe self-experiments in personal health. Demonstrated 19.20% improvement in experiment quality over traditional methods.

LLMs Rasa Self-Experimentation User Study

Digital Support for Dementia Caregivers

Large-Scale Community Analysis

Analyzed 100,000+ posts from Alzheimer's online communities to understand caregiver support needs. Built ML classifiers achieving AUC 0.83-0.87 for detecting emotional and informational support.

NLP Topic Modeling Classification Social Computing

Intelligent OCR for Medical Records

Document Digitization

Implemented a joint-learning framework using LayoutLMv1 for OCR and key-value extraction from 15,000+ handwritten medical documents. Achieved 0.983 accuracy on real-world healthcare forms.

Computer Vision OCR LayoutLM Healthcare

Technology Needs for Older Adults

Systematic Review & Focus Groups

Conducted systematic literature review and 3 focus groups with older adults to identify unmet technology needs in home settings. Developed evidence-based design recommendations for aging-in-place solutions.

Aging Focus Groups Literature Review Accessibility
